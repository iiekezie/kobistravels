Everything You Need to Know

Common questions students ask when planning studies in Australia.

Start from your strengths and long‑term career intent. Shortlist subject areas you genuinely enjoy, then map universities by graduate outcomes, industry integration and location (metro vs. regional). We help refine a realistic list and intake timeline.

Most universities accept IELTS Academic, PTE Academic, TOEFL iBT and sometimes Cambridge (C1 Advanced). Minimum scores differ by level and discipline. We review your profile and suggest the most strategic option and target scores.

International students may work limited hours during study periods and more during breaks. Part‑time roles build experience, offset living costs and expand networks—plan scheduling to protect academic performance.

Australian qualifications follow rigorous quality frameworks, making them portable for further study and professional licensing in many countries. Specific regulated professions may still require extra steps—ask us early.

Strong student support, research impact, industry placements and modern campus facilities. Many programs embed practical assessment and capstone projects that boost employability.

Typically academic transcripts, proof of English, a valid passport, CV (for some postgraduate programs), statement of purpose and references. Competitive courses may request portfolios or interviews.

Costs vary by city, level and discipline. Budget for tuition + accommodation + insurance + transport + study materials. We prepare a personalised cost breakdown and proof‑of‑funds guidance.
